REPORT CARD TEMPLATES

OWNED SKATEBOARDS

I absolutely love skateboarding, but buying decks gets expensive when you break them on a monthly basis. So, my solution was to try to start a Christian skateboard brand, with the idea that I would sell quality decks at a fair price to guys like me who love skating but aren't made of money. I had to put much of the initiative on hold due to job/life events, but I still plan to make it into something meaningful for the local skate community in the near future. Here's the website.
